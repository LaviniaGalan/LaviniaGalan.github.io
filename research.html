Research Interests

As an enthusiast in computer science, my research journey unfolds across various dimensions, each contributing to the dynamic landscape of technological innovation. I found constant fulfillment in studying every subject during my time at the faculty, enjoying the diverse intellectual challenges each course brought my way.


I am particularly interested in the following key domains:

Artificial Intelligence

My main interest lies in the exploration of artificial intelligence, where the objective is to combine computer science and robust datasets, to enable problem-solving. This involves delving into machine learning, neural networks, and natural language processing, with an emphasis on ethical considerations.

Performance Optimization

A central theme in my research involves enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of software and systems. This encompasses a meticulous examination of software architecture and resource utilization, with the ultimate goal of optimizing performance across diverse computing environments. Several machine learning techniques can be used in order to achieve this goal.

Data Analysis

In the era of expansive datasets, my focus on data analysis aims to unrevel meaningful insights from complex information. Employing statistical modeling, predictive analytics, and data-driven decision-making, I seek to extract valuable information from the patterns inherent in substantial datasets.

Financial Programming

Because of my profession, which lies at the intersection of finance and technology, I am dedicated to crafting innovative solutions through financial programming. This entails leveraging programming techniques to refine financial models, to implement algorithmic trading strategies and to fortify risk management protocols.

Secure Software Development

Considering the large amount of cybersecurity threats, my research extends to the enablement of secure software development. I am passionate about integrating security measures into the very fabric of software design and development processes, mitigating vulnerabilities from the beginning of the development process.
